Quick Start Guide

Enter the number of schools and number of rounds each team will play. If you want each team schedule or each room schedule on its own page check the respective box and then click "Go!". In the Teams box, a set of new fields will appear. Enter the name of the school and the number of teams from that school in each row. Once All the information is entered click "Generate Schedule." The program may take up to several minutes to generate a schedule. Once it is done, the result will appear on the screen. I would recommend printing the page to a PDF so you can email it to coaches and print specific pages later.

About
This website quickly generates schedules better than a person can. It ensures a team never plays the same team twice, and never plays a team from the same school all while creating randomness in schedules. All in all, it makes your tournament more fun, more fair, and less work.
